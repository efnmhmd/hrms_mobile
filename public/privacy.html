Privacy Policy

TalentShield HRMS  ·  Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected in the TalentShield HRMS mobile application (the “App”), provided by Vitrux (“we”, “us”, “our”). We are committed to protecting your personal data and handling it in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who this policy applies to

The App is a Human Resources Management System used by organisations to manage their workforce. It is intended for use by employees, managers, and administrators of organisations that have adopted TalentShield. In most cases your employer is the data controller for your HR records and we act as a data processor on their behalf; this policy describes how the App handles personal data.

2. Information we collect

  • Account & identity data: name, work email address, employee ID, job title, department, and phone number.
  • Authentication data: login credentials (passwords are stored only in hashed form) and session tokens used to keep you signed in.
  • HR & workforce data that you or your organisation provide, which may include: attendance and clock in/out records, shift and rota assignments, leave requests, expense claims and their attachments, performance objectives and reviews, and related employment information.
  • Device & technical data: device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and diagnostic/log data used to operate and secure the service.

The App does not collect your precise device location (GPS), and does not access your contacts, photos, or microphone except where you explicitly attach a file (e.g. an expense receipt).

3. How we use your data

  • To provide core HR functions (attendance, leave, rota, expenses, performance, approvals).
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the App.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Legal bases (UK GDPR): performance of your employment relationship/contract, our and your employer’s legitimate interests in managing the workforce, compliance with legal obligations, and, where applicable, your consent.

4. How your data is shared

  • Your organisation: your HR data is accessible to authorised personnel (e.g. managers, HR, administrators) within your employer, according to their role.
  • Service providers: hosting and infrastructure providers who process data on our behalf under appropriate contractual safeguards.
  • Legal requirements: where required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

We do not sell your personal data or use it for advertising.

5. Data retention

Personal data is retained for as long as your account is active and as required to provide the service, and thereafter as instructed by your organisation or as required to meet legal, accounting, or reporting obligations. When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymised.

6. Data security

All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. We apply role-based access controls, and passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request erasure of your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Because your employer is usually the data controller, some requests may be directed to them. We will assist your organisation in responding to such requests.

8. Requesting account or data deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, contact us at privacy@vitruxshield.com, or ask your organisation’s HR/administrator. We will process verified requests in line with applicable law and any retention obligations described above.

9. International data transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the UK or European Economic Area, we ensure an adequate level of protection through appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Children

The App is intended for use by employees and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
